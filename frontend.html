üî¨ Why Negative CO‚ÇÇ Emissions?

Negative emissions are scientifically valid!

When recycling credits exceed process emissions, the result is net negative CO‚ÇÇ. This happens when:
  • High recycled content (>50%)
  • Large recycling credits (aluminium: -14.5 kg CO‚ÇÇ/kg)
  • Efficient recycling processes
This means the recycled material saves more CO‚ÇÇ than the process creates.

Note: Recycling credits are negative (savings)
